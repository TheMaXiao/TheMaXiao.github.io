This paper presents an automated damage detection system for electromagnetic railgun launchers using advanced deep learning and computer vision techniques.

## Abstract

Electromagnetic railgun launchers require careful monitoring for internal damage. Our system combines deep learning with computer vision to provide automated, accurate damage detection.

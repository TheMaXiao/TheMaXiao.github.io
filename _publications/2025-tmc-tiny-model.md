This journal paper provides an extended and comprehensive analysis of tiny model selection techniques for microcontroller-based deployments, building upon our award-winning PerCom work.

## Abstract

This work presents a thorough investigation into the challenges and opportunities of deploying diverse machine learning models on microcontrollers, with extensive experimental validation and real-world case studies.

## Key Contributions

- Extended framework for tiny model deployment
- Comprehensive performance analysis across platforms
- Energy efficiency optimizations  
- Scalable deployment strategies
